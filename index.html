Privacy Policy

Developed by DevBySadik, the FajrFlow application prioritizes user privacy at the highest level.

1. Data Collection

Your personal data (name, email, password, etc.) is not collected or stored within the application. The app offers completely anonymous usage.

2. Location Information

Your approximate location data is used to calculate accurate prayer times and determine the Qibla direction. This data is processed locally for instant calculation and is never stored on our servers.
